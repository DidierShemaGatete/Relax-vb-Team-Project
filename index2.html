About Us

We are Relax Volleyball Team , a group of mainly former volleyball players, lovers, and families who are passionate about the sport and building strong relationships through it. Our team was established in 2010 with the aim of promoting volleyball and providing a platform for people to come together and enjoy the sport in a fun and relaxed environment.

Our team is open to players of all levels, from beginners to experienced players. We have regular training sessions and also participate in local volleyball tournaments and events. Our team is not just about playing volleyball, but also about building lasting friendships and creating a supportive community.

  • Summer Volleyball Camp

    June 12-16, 2023

    Location: Central High School Gymnasium

    Cost: $50 per person

    Description: This summer volleyball camp is designed for players of all skill levels who want to improve their skills and have fun playing volleyball. The camp will feature experienced coaches and players who will teach you the fundamentals of volleyball, including serving, passing, hitting, and setting. You'll also get to play in games and participate in fun drills and exercises.

  • Beach Volleyball Tournament

    July 23-24, 2023

    Location: Lakefront Beach Park

    Cost: $40 per team

    Description: This two-day beach volleyball tournament is open to all teams of two players. The tournament will be played in a double-elimination format, with each match consisting of three sets.
